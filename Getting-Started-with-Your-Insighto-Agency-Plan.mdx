# Insighto.ai Agency Plan Setup Guide

## 1  
Login to Insighto.ai account here: https://app.insighto.ai/login
![alt](images/Agency1.png)
## 2  
Click on Settings

## 3  
Click on Billing & Wallet

## 4  
Click on "For Agencies"

## 5  
Select the plan of your choice  
Click on Upgrade, it will redirect you to the billing portal -> Add Payment method and complete the purchase

## 6  
After the Purchase is complete, Click on Manage Agency

## 7  
Click on Getting started
