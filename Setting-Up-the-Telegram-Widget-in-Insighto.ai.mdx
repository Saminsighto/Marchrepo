### 1. Generate Your Telegram Bot Token

Before creating the widget in Insighto.ai, you need a Telegram bot token:

1. Open Telegram and search for [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather)
2. Start a chat and use the `/newbot` command
3. Follow the prompts to:
   - Choose a name for your bot
   - Choose a unique username (must end in `bot`, e.g., `support_assistant_bot`)

### 2. Access the Widgets Section in Insighto.ai

1. Log into your [Insighto.ai Dashboard](https://dashboard.insighto.ai)
2. Navigate to **AI Agents > Widgets**
3. Click on **Add a Widget**

---

### 3. Configure Your Telegram Widget

Fill in the following details:

- **Provider**: Select `Telegram`
- **Widget Type**: Choose the type of interaction (e.g., `Telegram Bot`)
- **Name**: Give your widget a clear, recognizable name (e.g., â€œTelegram Support Botâ€)
- **Description**: Add a brief internal description (optional)
- **Telegram Tool Config**: Select the telegram configuration you created in the tool section.

---

### 4. Link Your Assistant

- Select the **Assistant** you want to assign to this widget.
- This Assistant will handle all Telegram interactions using the knowledge and tone you've configured.

---

### 5. Finalize and Submit

Click **Submit** to complete the setup. Once the widget is created:

- Insighto.ai will start receiving and responding to messages sent to your Telegram bot.
- Conversations will be visible in the Inbox and logged under the AI Agent linked to the widget.
