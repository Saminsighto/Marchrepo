This guide will walk you through the process of setting up the **Call Transfer** and **Disconnect** tools on your platform. The tools can be integrated with your phone assistant, enabling users to transfer ongoing calls to a specific number or disconnect them altogether. You’ll learn how to configure these actions based on user commands or predefined conditions, ensuring seamless and efficient call handling.

## 🚫 Disconnect Tool Setup

1. Go to Tools & Integration & Click on Setup

From the left-hand sidebar of your Insighto dashboard, click on **Tools & Integration**.

Locate the **Disconnect Tool** and click **Set Up**.

2. Click on Start New

After clicking **Set Up**, a popup window will appear.

Click the **Start New** button to begin creating a new configuration.

3. Name Your Configuration and Click Create

Enter a name for your tool configuration.

Once you’ve entered the name, click **Create** to proceed.

4. Enable the Disconnect Call Function

In the configuration screen, toggle on the **Disconnect Call** function.

Click **Save** to finalize your tool setup. This will apply all your changes and complete the setup process.

5. Connect with Phone Assistant & Add Instructions to Trigger the Tool

Add logic to your assistant to determine when to use the disconnect tool.

**Instruction Example:**

> If the user doesn't have any more queries, thank them and then disconnect the call using the available tool.

## 🔁 Call Transfer Tool Setup

6. Go to Tools & Integration & Click on Setup

From the left-hand sidebar of your Insighto dashboard, click on **Tools & Integration**.

Locate the **Call Transfer Tool** and click **Set Up**.

7. Click on Start New

After clicking **Set Up**, a popup window will appear.

Click the **Start New** button to begin creating a new configuration.

8. Name Your Configuration and Click Create

Enter a name for your tool configuration.

Once you’ve entered the name, click **Create** to proceed.

9. Enable the Forward Call Function

In the configuration screen, toggle on the **Forward Call** function.

Click **Save** to proceed.

10. Enter the Number and Click on Finish

Enter the number where you want the call to be forwarded.

Click **Finish** to complete the setup.

11. Connect with Phone Assistant & Add Instructions to Trigger the Tool

Add logic to your assistant to determine when to use the call transfer tool.

**Instruction Example:**

> If the user requests to speak with (someone/Name), forward the call using the available tool.
